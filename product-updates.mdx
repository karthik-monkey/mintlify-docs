## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="What is Aida?">
  Aida is the AI associate for venture capital. Aida reviews startup materials, drafts memos, flags risks, and accelerates research — helping firms move faster from thesis to conviction.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does Aida work for VC firms?">
  You chat with Aida like you would with an analyst — ask questions about startups, markets, and materials, and get fast, structured responses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Aida secure for sensitive deal data?">
  Yes — all conversations and files are encrypted. Only you and your team can access your data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What kind of questions can I ask Aida?">
  You can ask Aida to summarize pitch decks, compare startups, draft memos, or flag red flags from uploaded materials.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use Aida on my phone or tablet?">
  Not yet — Aida currently works best on desktop for longer workflows and document-based tasks.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Does Aida help with writing memos or diligence notes?">
  Yes — Aida can generate first-draft investment memos, bullet-point diligence notes, and partner-ready summaries.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Will Aida support more advanced tools in the future?">
  Yes — we're actively building integrations, portfolio tracking, and deeper analysis features with VC feedback in mind.
</Accordion>
