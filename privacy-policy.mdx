## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Customer Information

When you sign up for or use the Services, we may collect:

- **Contact Details:** Name, email address, phone number, company/organization name, job title.
- **Account Credentials:** Username, password, and authentication tokens obtained via Google or GitHub.
- **Demographics & Preferences:** Optional information you choose to provide, such as your role, preferences, or profile picture.

### 1.2 End-User Data (Processed Locally)

As part of your use of our SDKs and APIs, end-user data—such as UI state, interaction patterns, feature usage metrics, error rates, performance data, and support conversation context—is processed in the user's browser. We do **not** store or share any personally identifiable end-user information; only anonymous, aggregated analytics are retained server-side.

### 1.3 Usage & Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information to operate and improve our Services:

- **Device & Browser Info:** IP address, browser type and version, operating system.
- **Log Data:** Server logs, web beacons, session duration, pages viewed, links clicked.
- **Cookies & Similar Technologies:** Cookies, local storage, encrypted refresh and access tokens.

### 1.4 Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from third parties who support our marketing, analytics, or payment processes. We use such data only to facilitate your experience and Service functionality.
